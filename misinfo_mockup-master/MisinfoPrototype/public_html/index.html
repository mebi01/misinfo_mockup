Why You Are Here.

Use of the Internet have resulted in heavier reliance on the Internet for disease and health education. 80% of American adult Internet users currently go online to find health care information for themselves and their loved ones. Existing medical and health forums offer this opportunity that people come together and talk about their health concerns and ask their questions and get advices/comment from peers. In this experiment, we have plan to understand how people can use this environment better. In the next pages, you see five events (scenarios) that are completely simulated (are not realistic) and we ask you to just suppose that you are in these simulated condition, and then answer some simple questions.
By taking part in this experiment you are going to play important role in helping people to use the internet for their health issues better than before. Congratulation! We highly appreciate your kind cooperation.
Before starting, let we have some basic information about you. You are not required to give name or last name or any other personal information and we do not need this kind of information.
